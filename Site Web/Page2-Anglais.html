His arrival in Monaco

He arrived at the age of 18 and his first professional contract

Due to the lack of a contract proposal from the Stade Rennais, Sofiane received numerous contract offers, including those from AS Monaco.

In a hurry to sign his first pro contract and our thanks to the Monaco project, Sofiane decides to settle in the principality.

He signed his first pro contract at the age of 18 and will therefore start training with the pros but will nevertheless start playing with the reserve team.
