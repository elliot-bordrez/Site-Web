His first pro matches and the arrival of Thierry Henry

Put in reserve team under Leonardo Jardim (Coach has the arrival of Sofiane Diop), Sofiane will make his big debuts with the pros following the appointment of Thierry Henry as a coach.

Henry will therefore believe in this young player and will nottament the launch in the Champions League on October 24, 2018 against Bruges

During the 2018-2019 season Sofiane Diop will play a few games with team A and acquire some playing time.

Unfortunately this will not last for him because Henry is dismissed after a bad result and the club will take back their former coach, Leonardo Jardim.
