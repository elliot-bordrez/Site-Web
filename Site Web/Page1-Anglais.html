His debut

His childhood as well as his junior career

Sofiane Diop born on 9 June 2000 in Tours is a professional footballer from France, he comes from both Senegal by his father, and Morocco by his mother.

Sofiane went to Jean-Giraudeau School and then to Corneille College. He was subsequently spotted, then went to the football U23 pole of Châteauroux.

In 2006, he joined the Tour FC club which is the first club of his career where he will stay 8 years.

Following his good performance under his first club of the Tours FC, he will join in 2014 the US Chambray-Lès-Tours.

During this season, Sofiane will be sparkling and will be recruited by the Ligue 1 club: Stade Rennais

He will therefore stay 3 years in Rennes before he decides to leave the club because he still had not received a pro contract offer.
